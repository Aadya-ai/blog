What's new ?

  • OUTDOOR CLASSROOM

    Welcome to the Heritage Elementary Outdoor Classroom! Our Outdoor Classroom was established in October 2019 by our Heritage students, teachers, and community. It includes several learning stations, such as box turtle habitat, wildflower meadow, sensory garden, frog and toad bog, pollinator garden, butterfly garden, caterpillar garden, songbird sanctuary, cardinal directions sign, and weather station. The outdoor classroom is maintained by students and community members and used for hands on education and environmental learning during the school year. We invite our community members to enjoy our outdoor classroom, and ask that you please treat the grounds with care and respect.

  • MCS Schedule Next Week

    Beginning Tuesday, January 19, after the Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. holiday, all Madison City Schools students who chose school-based learning will return to our schools for every day instruction.
